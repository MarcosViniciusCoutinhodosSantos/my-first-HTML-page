Synopsis

How has visual kei changed the lives of young Brazilians in the city of Juiz de Fora?

This page has the purpose of testing HTML and CSS

The production tells the story of Brazilian young adults which decided to start bands and to write songs in the Japanese language, therefore developing a very uncommon and interesting local music scene totally inspired by the Visual Kei and J-Rock in the city of Juiz de Fora.

Directed by Leonardo Amorim
Script/Research by Marcos Vinícius
Graphic Art by Gabriel Patrocínio

Interviwees

Andrey Almeida
Luis Filipe Proença
Pablo Narada
Rodrigo Telles
Thais Fávero
Thiago Guarnieri

Short documentary, Full HD Digital, color, 2017, 20 min length.
